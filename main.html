Baahubali

An Epic Indian Historical Action Film

Actors Favorite Quote
Baahubali tells the story of Shivudu, a brave young man who discovers his royal heritage. He learns about the legendary warrior Amarendra Baahubali and the conspiracy that led to his death. The movie follows themes of courage, sacrifice, justice, and the battle for the kingdom of Mahishmati.

Actors

The movie stars Prabhas as Amarendra Baahubali and Mahendra Baahubali, Rana Daggubati as Bhallaladeva, Anushka Shetty as Devasena, Tamannaah Bhatia as Avantika, Ramya Krishnan as Sivagami, and Sathyaraj as Kattappa.
